# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Participants in this project are expected to:

- be respectful and assume good intent
- give technical feedback that is specific and actionable
- keep discussion focused on the work
- avoid harassment, insults, and discriminatory behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- personal attacks or abusive language
- harassment, intimidation, or stalking
- sexualized language or imagery
- publication of private information without permission
- deliberate disruption of project discussions or reviews

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers
through GitHub and include links or screenshots when relevant. Reports will be
reviewed privately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or
other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.
